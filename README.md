## 🏢 Core Consulting Focus Areas

### 🛒 FMCG & Retail Margin Intelligence
*   **The Problem:** Retailers growing top-line volume while leaking bottom-line profit due to unoptimized unit economics and slow-moving stock.
*   **The Solution:** Custom inventory-velocity and product-rationalization engines that isolate low-margin SKUs and optimize regional warehouse distribution.

### 🏥 Operations Capacity & Throughput Optimization
*   **The Problem:** Capacity bottlenecks, inefficient staffing ratios, and missed service levels (SLAs) inflating organizational overhead.
*   **The Solution:** Performance and resource-allocation models that streamline workflow velocity and align operational capacity with real-time demand.

### 📉 Customer Retention & Revenue Protection
*   **The Problem:** Unidentified customer churn eroding predictable recurring revenue streams.
*   **The Solution:** Early-warning churn signaling pipelines and predictive segmentation models to protect predictable top-line revenue.
